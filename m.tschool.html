Secondary

A secondary school is an educational institution where students typically progress after primary school, focusing on more advanced academic subjects like mathematics, science, humanities, and languages, while also providing opportunities for specialized learning through electives and extracurricular activities, allowing teenagers to further develop their skills and prepare for higher education or vocational training.
